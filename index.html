About Me

Xiaohui Huang

I am a Ph.D. student at CISE (The Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering), the University of Florida. I joined UF Modern Artificial Intelligence and Learning Technologies (MALT) Lab and have worked with Prof. Sanjay Ranka and Prof. Anand Rangarajan since 2016. My research interests are mainly about video processing and spatial computing in computer vision and machine learning. Before I came to UF, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering from Sichuan University, China, 2015.
