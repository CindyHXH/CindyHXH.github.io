I am a Ph.D. student at CISE (The Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering), University of Florida. I am supervised by Prof. Sanjay Ranka and Prof. Anand Rangarajan. My research interests are video and spatiotemporal processing in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning. Before I came to UF, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering in 2015 from Sichuan University, China.
