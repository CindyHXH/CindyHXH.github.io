Xiaohui Huang

I am a Ph.D. student at CISE (The Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering), the University of Florida. I am supervised by Prof. Sanjay Ranka and Prof. Anand Rangarajan. My research interests are about video processing and spatial computing in computer vision and machine learning. Before I came to UF, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering from Sichuan University, China, 2015.
